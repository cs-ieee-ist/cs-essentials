About

CS Essentials is a website that helps students learn and practice with CS-related tools, for example, Vim, Gdb, and Git. Our goal is to help students being more proficient and efficient when using these tools. Our goal is to provide a platform that anyone can use to learn and even use as a companion when doing using one of the tools.

IEEE IST

The IEEE IST Student Branch is an autonomous associative group from Instituto Superior Técnico, which branches from the global organization IEEE and focus in developing innovative initiatives and events targeting fields that are missing from the academic education, ranging from technical skills’ teaching to providing the best opportunities for internships. To know more about us visit our website.
